# Our Review Methodology

## How We Test Products

Every review on DealsMyTech follows a standardized testing protocol to ensure 
consistency and reliability.

### VPN Testing Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Testing Method |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| Speed Performance | 25% | Multiple server tests using Speedtest.net |
| Security Features | 25% | Protocol analysis, kill switch testing |
| Privacy Policy | 20% | Legal review, jurisdiction analysis |
| Ease of Use | 15% | UI/UX evaluation, setup process |
| Value for Money | 15% | Price per month, feature comparison |

### Product Rating Scale

- **9-10**: Exceptional - Best in class
- **7-8.9**: Very Good - Recommended with minor caveats
- **5-6.9**: Average - Acceptable but alternatives exist
- **Below 5**: Not Recommended

### Testing Environment

- Location: Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia
- Connection: TM Unifi 500Mbps baseline
- Devices: Windows 11, macOS, Android, iOS
- Testing Period: Minimum 2 weeks per product

### Independence Statement

Manufacturers cannot pay for favorable reviews. We purchase products retail 
or use reviewer units with no editorial strings attached.

## AFFILIATE MANAGER RED FLAGS TO AVOID
================================================================================

### Immediate Rejection Triggers

✗ Free subdomain (blogspot, wordpress.com, wix.com)
✗ Placeholder content ("Lorem ipsum", "Coming soon")
✗ Stock photos with watermarks
✗ No contact information
✗ Missing privacy policy
✗ Thin content (articles under 500 words)
✗ Copied content from other sites
✗ No clear niche focus
✗ Broken links or 404 errors
✗ Slow loading website
✗ No SSL certificate
✗ Pop-up heavy design
✗ Auto-playing media

### Yellow Flags (May Delay Approval)

⚠ Fewer than 10 articles
⚠ No author information
⚠ Generic "admin" author name
⚠ No social media presence
⚠ Very new domain (<3 months)
⚠ No traffic data (some programs check)
⚠ Inconsistent publishing schedule
